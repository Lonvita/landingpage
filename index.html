The business

LONVITA.IO is a data-driven health and longevity platform that transforms wearable and lifestyle data into personalised, actionable routines. The system combines AI-powered analytics with evidence-based recommendations across movement, recovery, nutrition, and longevity. Users receive daily guidance tailored to their goals, physiology, and habits. The platform targets health-conscious individuals, athletes, and people aiming to improve long-term wellbeing through measurable, science-based interventions.

Product Stage

The platform is in active development with core features already functional: wearable integrations, AI-based recommendations, and a working marketplace connection. Pilot partnerships with hotels and wellness providers are underway. The next development milestone includes deeper physiological modelling, expanded device integrations, and a refined user interface for the consumer app.

Services

  1. AI Longevity Mentor An intelligent engine that analyses wearable data, lifestyle patterns, and personal goals to generate daily guidance and routine suggestions.
  2. Health & Performance Dashboard Unified view of sleep, activity, biomarkers (where available), habits, and progress trends.
