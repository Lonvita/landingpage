# Landingpage

Create a website that has the form of a landingpage. The landingpage consists of only one page and is composed of the following parts:

## Header

The header is full width and has a background image. The background image is found in ./assets/images/1.jpg has a light blue CSS filter and is desaturated via CSS. Additionially, there's a logo in the middle of the header container, which is located in ./assets/images/logo.jpeg. Underneath the logo there's a slogan, formatted as prominent text "Your personal AI Mentor for Longevity, Nutrition & Sports". Under the slogan there's a paragraph which contains the text "LONVITA.IO combines science, wearable data, and daily guidance to optimize your health and performance."

## Main Section

The main section has a beige background color and contains the title "How it works", horizontally centered. Underneath is a paragraph that contains the text "Three simple steps to get personalized, AI powered insights" in 1.1rem font-size. Under that there are 3 boxes with white background that contain icons (yet to be defined) and the titles "Connect", "AI Analysis" and "Actionable Insights". They all contain a paragraph as well, the first one "Connect your personal health application from your smartphone with LONVITA.IO", the second "The more data you share, the better LONVITA can guide you", and the third "LONVITA builds your personalized daily routine from your data, adapts to your needs".
